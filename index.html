Our Mission

There are 815 correctional facilities in India, housing thousands of underprivileged children that do not get the personal attention they deserve and need, to help them get back a normal life in the society. Lack of funding and a trained staff means that these children lack the resources to break out of a system that has failed them and that they are at a disadvantage to peers who have the access to technology and the internet. With the current virus rampaging, it becomes more important that we help them gain access to the online world. We are partnering with Headstreams NGO in order to achieve our goals.
